Frequently asked questions 

​​Our current architecture is poorly documented. Can Zenblox help us map out our current state as part of the engagement?

Yes. Zenblox conducts a structured discovery process to understand and document your current architecture. This helps establish a clear baseline and ensures all stakeholders are aligned before any new design decisions are made.

​​What factors does Zenblox consider during a solution architecture assessment?

We evaluate your architecture across multiple dimensions, including system scalability, performance, security, compliance, integration capabilities, and alignment with your business goals. We also assess risks, technical debt, and opportunities for modernization to ensure solutions are practical, future-proof, and cost-effective.

​​​If gaps or risks are identified, can Zenblox help us address them?

Absolutely. Once gaps or risks are identified, we provide actionable recommendations and hands-on guidance to remediate them. This can include improving security controls, optimizing system performance, updating compliance measures, or redesigning processes to reduce long-term risk.

​​Can Zenblox help us validate a vendor’s proposed solution or quote?

Yes. Zenblox can independently review vendor proposals to ensure technical feasibility, cost-effectiveness, and compliance with your requirements. We provide expert guidance so you can make informed decisions and avoid overpaying or adopting suboptimal solutions.

​​We’re under pressure to deliver quickly—can Zenblox still support us if we’re already mid-project?

Yes. Zenblox can integrate into ongoing projects to provide rapid assessment, recommendations, and support. Our team works with your existing project timelines and resources, ensuring that improvements or corrections are delivered efficiently without disrupting progress.
